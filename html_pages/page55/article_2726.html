Rurouni Kenshin: Special techniques is a special video that goes through all the techniques the Hiten Mitsurugi Ryuu style has to offer.
We have seen these throughout the anime but a cool explanation and showcase of each one is pretty cool.

The anime's story is rather touching with the Japanese revolution and the life of Kenshin. 9/10

The art is the same as in the anime (9) and the voice acting is fine (7), the characters are truly unique and really fun too, 9.

Overall this episode was pretty cool but it is just 1 episode with a single theme and reused frames.8/10
