Well, for a moment there I thought the OVA will be just another fan service.

Turns out the OVA of Witch Craft Works itself, in my opinion, generate more excitement for me than the actual series itself. It is very light-hearted and to be frank, the comedy is very refreshing.

Bringing the bro-con topic of the main character's sister and WWE-ish featuring bear and penguin, the OVA is just an extension to the main series.

The art and the sound are very much the same with the usual series. The character however, stole the spotlight in this OVA.

I have to say that the OVA is much more enjoyable and comedic compared to the main series.

For overall resultt, this is one OVA that is good, if not, better than the main series in term of enjoyment.

Really not much to say about this.

An extra, self-contained episode focusing on Kasumi and tanpopo. It's nothing amazing, but it's enjoyable. It has all the quality of the main series, except it had a bit more humour and action(which makes sense considering it's a standalone episode) I disagree with the reviewer who said it's better than the main series. I'd say this episode was nice, but a bit lower quality than the main series ones. On that note, I was sort of disappointed it didn't tie into the story at all or add much characterisation.

Anyways, if you liked WCW, I'd recommend watching this.
