That view of the pink-haired girl’s naked body is unreal. White Fox Studio deserves an award and an obligatory wank from each person who watches this show. I’ll give you a moment if you’re still not finished…….

…………………………………..**looks at watch**……………………**later realizes that he does not own a watch**………………

Due to the roaring success of Goblin Slayer, it prompted the producers to encapsulate the best elements of the series in a concise package for viewer’s who were not previously aware of the Goblin Slayer’s exploits, to catch up on all of the hype that surrounds this triumphant achievement. For the regulars, it serves as a veritable highlight reel to heighten the anticipation before the final two episodes.

Numerous viewers associate recap episodes with tedious affairs, but let me tell you something: they are not. They are essential in reviewing the main plot points, as well as showcasing the awesome talents of the Goblin Slayer. One cannot understand how adept the Goblin Slayer is at slaying goblins, until they watch a recap of Goblin Slayer slaying the same goblins. Certifiable fact.

Let’s be honest, though: this recap suffered from an abundance of dialogue and not enough goblin slaying. I was expecting, at least, 80% of the episode to consist of goblin curb-stomping, but this simply was not the case. Don’t get it twisted, this recap had some wonderful moments of the Goblin Slayer slicing and dicing; smashing and bashing; chop-choping and molly-wopping; torching and scorching; and….I’m outta sublime rhymes, just watch this Goblin Slayer recap during your Christmas gift wrap.

Final thoughts…

The dark, murky artwork captures the essence of the ruthless goblins, instilling a level of fear and trepidation that feels palpable. The OP hits the right tone of being somber, yet energetic and vivacious. Even the naysayers must admit to themselves (in the dark recesses of their mind) that the awesomeness of the Goblin Slayer cannot be denied. In time, they will learn, just as the adventurers in Goblin Slayer will learn the significance of killing goblins — the most essential job there is to prevent ceaseless rape and crime. Is that what you want?…Cause that’s what you’ll get without the Goblin Slayer.
