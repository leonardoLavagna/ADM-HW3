Synopsis

Sarah is a most terrible pirate knight who dances her way around the galaxy empire that has been ravaged by war. In the galaxy ruled by a number of power-hungry rival warlords, an evil sadistic web is woven around Sarah, who is slowly guided down to her destruction. When the silver knight Sylia comes across the evil aristocrats who have run the gamut of corruption in the galaxy and the slave traders of darkness, the demonic twins, a desperate predicament is visited upon Sarah!!

Not to mention that when you are working an adaptation of a Lilith Soft game, you will expect nothing but god-tier quality.

Lilith Soft, also known for the original game Taimanin Asagi, has an art style that is too fantastic to ignore. Both seiyuus' performances are done very well, especially Emi Motoi as Sara, who gives it her all in the sex scenes. The two main antagonists are two sexy lolis, Cecil and Karin, that possess sadistic qualities that you do not see enough of in hentai. The scene involving them and the two heroines after both of them turned into futanari is one of my absolute favorite hentai scenes. One other scene, in particular, involves Sara and Sylia being publically humiliated in front of a bourgeois crowd is a close 2nd to being the best scene. However, the scenes are not exactly anything special. The final does involve a lot of tentacle action, but me being someone who does not have a significant affinity towards that kind of fetish did not garner any big reaction.
