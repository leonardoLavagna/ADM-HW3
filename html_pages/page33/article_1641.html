Honestly I was not expecting to get an anime adaptation of Obey me! as otome mobile games and the like often don't get turned into anime. So, needless to say I was really excited about this one.

Obviously, you can't really hold this show up to the same standard as regular-length anime since each episode is much shorter, only being a few minutes at most. Because of this, there's not really an overarching plot, instead it's more of an episodic show focusing on comedy and featuring the characters from the game and their interactions with one another. I don't think that this by any means is a bad thing though, since, if you've played the game, you know that the characters are the best part of it! I could honestly easily watch hours of these brothers and their shenanigans! Their just so fun to watch, you know?

Not gonna lie though, it would have been fun to see a regular full-length show, with a more serious tone and legit story, exploring the characters more. Perhaps it'll happen in the future? For now, I'm perfectly content with this.

Surprisingly, the animation, artstyle and overall quality of the show is really good. The artstyle looks just like the game so I'm really happy about it!

Overall, a really good adaptation! I definitely recommend to any fan of the game!

Hello, do you like demon boys being wholesome? Well that’s what this anime is. Pretty much it’s just the obey me brothers’ interactions from day to day. (This is my first time ever writing a review, so go easy on me please!)

Story: 5
There’s no story, just different scenarios happening each episode. I personally don’t like that style, but it was done okay here. Each episode was somewhat unique.

Art: 6
The animation is pretty smooth, it’s what you’d see in game for the cards. The art looks very soft and cloudy.I think the art looks alright.

Sound: 8
I never pay much attention to sound, but they did great. The VAs did amazing here. The OP and ED was absolutely amazing. I just think everyone did great here and that whoever made the OP and ED did well.

Character: 7
I think that most of the characters were likable here. Everyone was different, so that everyone can find a character they like.

Enjoyment: 6
I did enjoy this at times. I think that most the parts were boring to be honest. I just don’t think anime like this is my thing. I perfer overarching stories, and this is just a short anime. I still did enjoy most of it though.

Overall: 6
I think this anime is good for when you are bored or just want something short to watch. It’s an okay anime for fans who were looking forward to an anime (me). Overall, it’s alright, not close to perfect, but just alright.

Obey me is a mobile otome game of "which will you pick" cool demon boy style.

I have never played that game but just five minutes of the show will tell you that. They know their target audience so it's not really a show so much as otome fan service.

Each episode starts out with a random sketch, the boys look super cute interacting with each other and putting on different outfits, the episode ends. That's really it, eye candy for the fans with a lot of inside jokes and references.


The art is what sells this one but it's pretty empty otherwise. There is no plot or characters, they really are there as eye candy. The one negative is the voice acting which at times makes it seem like the person is pinching their nose while saying the lines.

It's not a bad show with each "episode" barely clocking in at 5 minutes. It's just kindda boring unless you've played the game and already know the characters.
