The story revolves around two characters namely, Sonomura Ayako and Narasaki Akito. It starts withi Akito moves from a place to live with Ayako. Yes. Two of them only. Ayako's husband has already been dead long time ago! So, we know that Ayako is older than Akito for quite some age.

She has been alone since her husband died. Meaning a woman sexual desire for a man has rose to whichever peak she has. She can no longer hold it.

The moment when Akito lives in with her. She feels very grateful and excited. So, she who is the only host of the house decided to cook some sumptuous meal for him. She does it as a way of welcoming Akito. Their meal consists of alcohol as the beverages. After taking in some alcohol, she was drunken and began to say something very weird like 'Akito are you a virgin'? Then, she did what she yearn as a widow.

The next day, she apologizes to Akito for her misbehave. Being drunken is the reason she gave. Then, Akito also expresses his feeling for Ayako. He loves her. At the end of the story Ayako embraced her own feelings towards Akito.

The story I gave it a 8/10. It is a good watch.

I would say that it is unique although the route is quite predictable, But, it gives some brand new sense of living with a widow!
