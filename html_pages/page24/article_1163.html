Synopsis

Ichiro Mizusawa, a player from Rukawa's old junior high school, Tomigoaka, is diagnosed with a crippling leg condition and wants to play one last game with Rukawa. Hanamichi sets out to help the boy and fulfill his wish.

The best slam dunk movie out of the 4. The art and sound were good as usual similar to that of the series. I think this Slam Dunk movie really shined in the story and the character area. I realized that even though Rukawa is harsh and mean at times at the end of the day he's just a competitor and is a good guy. Also the story was amazing with the trials and tribulations an athlete who has suffered a devastating injury has to go through such as the mental and physical turmoil. It was really well shown.

Overall a very enjoyable and also an emotional Slam Dunk movie. Man I need to forget this movie if I wanna experience it again, I can't put it in words!
