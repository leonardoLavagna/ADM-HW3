Synopsis

Keiichi Oyamada has a mysterious power that always makes him seem like a lucky pervert, as one way or another, he ends up having sexual intercourse with any female he meets. Due to this, he has never had a stable job as he continually gets fired from any place unfortunate enough to employ him.

One job opportunity arises when his best friend calls him to work at a manga cafe, which Keiichi reluctantly accepts. However, as soon as he starts his new job, he meets Rei Suzukawa, a fellow employee, and Kururu Hiiragi, his childhood friend. Both of them are very beautiful, and Keiichi fears that even they might not be spared by his lucky perverted powers.

Comedic Gold for a Hentai. In fact, Mankitsu Happening LITERALLY means Comedic Happenings. Mankitsu Happening has all the elements of a good hentai: amazing art, dynamic animations, sufficient personality, and a solid premise.

The premise for the story is the Male MC (Keiichi) is down on his luck cause he keeps fucking people by accident. It's like Rito Yuuki, from To Love-RU, ascended to another dimension. The bizarre premise allows Keiichi to slip into some fancy situations--or should I say slip into some fancy women.
( ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°)

The only issue someone could have with the story is that it is unbelievable. Despite being a completely realistic hentai, it's about as ridiculous as tentacle porn. If this bothers the viewer, then there could be some major issues since trying to find justification for anything in this hentai is going to quickly make your libido go south.

However, if one can suspend that disbelief and focus on the valuable things like the poses and the scenarios the characters undergo, then the hentai truly shines.

Mankitsu Happening uses subtle background music to stimulate feelings of suspense, ease, and excitement. The subtle use of the music essentially shifts the priority towards the amazing voice acting. It's also quite surprising how well the silent bgm is utilized so as to not detract focus from a situation or to lead up to events.

The characters are one-dimensional, but they undergo dynamic changes in their actions as they encounter new events. These reactions are enough to justify the void of how meaningful each character is as it parallels with the premise of the story. Keiichi makes perverted woopsies and the women react by escalating it to sex. Even so, while the characters may be flat in their personality, they certainly make up for it with how "round" their plot is.
( ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°)

The situations are exciting and the buildup is hysterical. If you like silly things and fapping to it, this happens to be the hentai for you.

While I definitely enjoyed this hentai a lot, it ended up frustrating me a little bit.

In terms of presentation, the series keeps up with Collaboration Works' high standard. The studio did a really good job transferring the VN art which was not particularly great into animation. All scenes are pretty well animated, though there is some obvious looping.
There are also many ahegao faces, so if you don't like those you won't enjoy this as much.
Also, as you'd expect from Collab Works title, there are several not very subtle references to other works by the same game company, such as posters and cameo appearances. Some of them were even made by a different studio.

The story is pretty simple vanilla stuff. We have a guy called Keiichi who possesses some kind of mysterious ability. He manages to seduce and bang every girl he meets, which got him into trouble and fired from every previous job. Now he works at a manga cafe and really hopes not to get fired this time. But as expected, he gets into various awkward situations again.

One thing that I didn't expect from this anime was that there were several genuinely funny moments. You just don't see these often. I'm obviously not gonna spoil them, but the particularly hilarious moment was the one in first episode where the protagonist trips over in the bathroom and gets a blowjob from Rei.

The characters are also simple, but varied enough. I wasn't a huge fan of Kururu, I am just allergic to girls that shout the phrase "ONIIIII-CHAN!" and her scenes were by far the weakest.
Rei on the other hand was simply great, if your fetishes are MILFs, anal and milking, she will fulfil all of them. I am not a particular fan of these, but the hilarious scenes combined with her stoic personality made her the standout character.
But my favourite character by far was the idol Otona, not only because I personally found her the hottest, but because her introduction where she tried to chase the protagonist was hilarious.

In my opinion, the second episode might be one of the greatest hentai episodes ever. In the first half the hot blonde idol shows up and gets some action and in the second half we get a massive orgy which involves anal, breast sucking, double blowjob and more. I watched that episode at least 10 times already.

But the series is not without its problems, the next 2 episodes are significantly weaker, the third one just contains several stealthy scenes including one in the cupboard and more anal sex.
And the 4th one is just pointless, a new girl get introduced that owns a bigger manga shop, but her scenes were really mediocre and she doesn't even have sex with the protagonist.
These episodes were definitely rushed since they came out only a month apart from each other.

In case they announce another episode, I really hope we get more funny moments and awesome group scenes.

But despite the problems, I rate this OVA very highly and consider it the best vanilla hentai of the year with Lovely x Cation.

And finally, my official best girl ranking:
Otona > Rei >> Kururu >>> Sayaka
